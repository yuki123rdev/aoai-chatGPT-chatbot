CancelOrRefund API​

Payment MethodMemo
Credit CardCancel
  • The period for cancellation is within 50 days after the payment transaction is authorized.
Refund
  • The period allowed for the first refund is within 390 days after the transaction is captured. Then the period allowed for other refunds is within 390 days after the latest succeeded refunded request. (i.e. it extends 390 days after each successful refund)
