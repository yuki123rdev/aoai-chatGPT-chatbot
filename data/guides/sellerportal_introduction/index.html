What is Seller Portal? and Main functions.​

  • The Seller Portal screen consists of the following three screens.

The Seller Portal screen allows you to search and check your transaction history.
You can cancel transaction details, change the amount, etc., from this screen.

This is a management tool to handle payment transactions created by Rakuten Card Payment System.


Functions​

Search functions​

  • You can search for payment history with exact matches in the following fields.
  • You can also narrow down your search by multiple items.

  • You can search by period.


Search Results​

  • Search results are displayed on the screen.
  • You can check your payment ID, payment amount, payment status, etc.


Search Results(Payment Details)​

  • Clicking on the statement will display the Payment Details screen.
  • Please check the error code and other details as necessary. Please refer to the Seller Portal User Manual for detailed instructions.

Payment Transaction Management(Modify・Capture・Cancel or Refund)​

  • You can change the amount or perform other processes from the Payment Details screen.
  • Processing must be done on a statement-by-statement basis.
  • Please refer to the next and beyond for details on each process.


Payment Transaction Management(Modify)​

  • “Modify" changes the amount of a payment transaction.
  • It can also be used as a reauthorization to extend the “authorized” (provisional sales) status period.
  • The amount can also be changed for transactions that are in the status of “captured”(actual sales).

Payment Transaction Management(Capture)​

  • “Capture" puts a payment transaction that is in the status of “authorized” into the status of “captured”.

Payment Transaction Management(Cancel or Refund)​

  • “Cancel or Refund” cancels a payment transaction that is in the status of an authorization or sales confirmation.


CSV of Payments download function​

  • You can output the settlement transaction details as a CSV file.
  • Please use this function to check the details against your company's data.
note

Period when output is possible: Output is possible for the entire period.
Number of statements that can be output at one time: Unlimited
